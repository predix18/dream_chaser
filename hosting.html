Understanding Hosting Options

Choosing the right hosting solution is crucial for your website's performance and scalability. Here are the most common options:

Shared Hosting

Perfect for small websites and beginners. Your site shares server resources with other websites.

  • Affordable
  • Easy to set up
  • Managed maintenance
  • Good for low traffic
$3-$10/month

VPS Hosting

Virtual Private Server offers dedicated resources within a shared environment.

  • More power & flexibility
  • Root access
  • Scalable resources
  • Better security
$20-$50/month

Cloud Hosting

Your website is hosted on a network of servers, offering maximum reliability and scalability.

  • High availability
  • Pay for what you use
  • Automatic scaling
  • Enterprise-grade
$10-$100+/month

What's Next for Your Project?

For your School Wall of Fame website, I recommend starting with a shared hosting provider like Bluehost or SiteGround. They offer:

  • One-click WordPress installation (if you want to convert later)
  • Free domain name for the first year
  • 24/7 support to help with any issues
  • Easy-to-use control panel

Your website is currently static HTML/CSS/JS, which makes it easy to host on any provider. Simply upload all your files to the public_html folder of your hosting account.
